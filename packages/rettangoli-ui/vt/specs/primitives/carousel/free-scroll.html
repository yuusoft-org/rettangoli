--- title: Carousel - Free Scroll - Snap Disabled description: Verifies the carousel primitive can disable scroll snapping while keeping drag and button navigation available. specs: - '`snap="false"` disables native post-scroll snapping.' - 'Mouse drag and trackpad scrolling can stop between slides.' - 'Built-in arrow controls still scroll to the target slide.' --- Free-scroll carousel Use this when the strip should feel more like a rail than a locked carousel. Dragging and wheel scrolling can stop between cards. Slide 1 Snap disabled. Release the drag anywhere and the strip stays where you left it. Slide 2 Arrow controls still target slides. The built-in left and right controls still scroll intentionally to each item. Slide 3 More rail-like behavior. This is useful for landing-page content strips where partial positions can still feel natural.
